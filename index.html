About Me

I'm an AI/ML Scientist at Siemens Healthineers, developing scalable AI solutions for whole-body oncology applications, including (1) foundation models, and (2) annotation-efficient sample selection (active learning) and modeling (in-context learning).

In my spare time, I enjoy participating in international AI challenges where researchers compete in fair and rigorous benchmarking environments. Through these competitions, I have developed several novel algorithms (e.g., PanDx and AnyMC3D) that achieved 1st places across core medical imaging tasks, including classification, segmentation, synthesis, and detection. These results reflect my research philosophy of pursuing simple, implementable methods that deliver strong practical results.
