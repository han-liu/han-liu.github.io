Biography [CV]

I am a fourth year (2022-2023) Ph.D. student in Computer Science, Vanderbilt University, supervised by Prof. Benoit Dawant and Prof. Ipek Oguz. Previously, I received the B.S degree from Department of Biomedical Engineering and Electrical Engineering in Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in 2016, mentored by Prof. Ge Wang. In 2017, I received the M.S degree from the Department of Biomedical Engineering in Yale University, advised by Prof. James Duncan. Before joining Vanderbilt, I worked as a research associate in the Imaging Research Lab in the Department of Radiology in University of Pittsburgh, advised by Prof. Jiantao Pu.

My research interests lie in the fields of machine learning and medical image analysis. I'm especially interested in developing techniques for incomplete data scenarios. Currently, I'm working on an active learning projects.
